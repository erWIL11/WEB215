"Green-thumb" Complex

Each month we will celebrate the growth in the Love Luminance Community Garden by putting on several events. These special events will not only highlight the progress of our garden but also add a social element. Growing vegetables to place healthy options at the dinner table, is now a convenient option. We encourage parents to bring their kids, to get them familiar with many varieties of vegetables. The community garden will also have varieties of herbs and flowers to bring our garden full circle. The elements created throughout the garden inspires our special events. Our canvas (garden) has exponential potential of lush foliage and veggies.

Love Luminance Community Garden provides a way for people to serve the surrounding communities and the environment. Carving out spaces to grow garden enhances the soil and creates great ecosystems for pollination. Not to mention, gardening is considered to be therapeutic. Each month we will update the event postings. We also send out pretty digital flyers, straight to your inbox.

If you know a company or run one, and you like to sponsor an event contact us!
