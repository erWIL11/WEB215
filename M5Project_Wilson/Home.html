Purpose


A place where a common purpose of community can flourish. Our focus is providing
a space to embrace gardening skills while also incorporating healthy eating.

We encourage everyone to come out and spend as little as ten minutes of your day
to help with a little planting. Whether you are taking your daily walk or just having
a family day outing, stop by to see your community garden's progress.

Everyone is welcome to the yield of your Garden. As with most things in
life just keep others in mind. No food goes to waste in our Garden, so donating
fresh vegetable is our way of helping others in need. Each season brings a variety
of "in-season" vegetable and flowers, so let us know your needs and ideas.
